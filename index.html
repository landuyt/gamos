Paxos

According to Greek mythology, Poseidon created Paxos by striking Corfu with his trident, so that he and his wife Amphitrite could have some peace and quiet. Unfortunately for them, on 28 August we will be making some noise.



More on Paxos

The three key settlements on Paxos are Gaios, Lakka and Longos. Gaios, as the capital and main port, is the center of the island’s life: most restaurants, cafés and shops are found here. Lakka, as the yacht owners’ favourite, is more cosmopolitan while Longos is a picturesque fishing village. It does not matter which one you pick as your base: Paxos is not a big island, merely covering a surface of 19 square kilometers.
